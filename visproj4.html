Sunset Over Ocean - Animation

This project presents a visually immersive animation of a sunset over the ocean, capturing the subtle beauty of natural transitions in a coastal setting. The scene includes a setting sun that gradually lowers from a bright yellow to a deep red as it nears the horizon, with a color-shifting sky that transitions from blue to warm hues of orange and pink. This effect is achieved through gradient blending that adjusts based on the sunâ€™s position, simulating the changing light of evening.

The ocean waves are created using multiple sine waves layered together, each with different amplitudes and frequencies. This method emulates the complex, rolling nature of real ocean waves, with slight offsets providing depth and movement across the surface. To enhance realism, a foamy edge is added to the peaks of the waves, mimicking the way light catches the tips of ocean swells as the sun sets. The ocean itself darkens progressively, reflecting the shift to twilight.

Clouds float across the sky, varying in color from bright white to warm sunset shades. Positioned randomly in the upper part of the canvas, they drift smoothly from one side of the screen to the other, adding to the sceneâ€™s dynamism. Their colors and positions are updated to reflect the changing ambiance of the sunset.

This project highlights techniques in procedural animation, color gradients, and complex wave simulation for a cohesive natural scene. View the complete code and project details on my GitHub Repository.
