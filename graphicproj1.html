Bright Mind International Academy

This project is a comprehensive showcase of my graphic design skills, highlighting a range of materials created for branding and administrative purposes. Each element has been meticulously crafted using Canva and Adobe Illustrator to ensure both aesthetic appeal and practical functionality.

Logo Design: The logo reflects the brand identity with a clean, modern aesthetic that conveys professionalism and approachability. The design process involved several iterations to ensure the final logo is both memorable and versatile for use across different mediums.

Letterhead Design: The letterhead was designed to provide a cohesive and professional look for official correspondence. It incorporates the logo and maintains a consistent visual language with carefully chosen typography and layout.

Admission Form: The admission form is designed with user experience in mind, ensuring it is easy to understand and complete. The layout is clean, with a logical flow that minimizes confusion, making it effective for administrative use.

Report Card: The report card design emphasizes clarity and structure, ensuring that all important information is easy to read at a glance. The visual design balances formal presentation with approachable styling, suitable for an educational setting.

Compliance Documents: Two documents have been prepared for compliance purposes: an undertaking and an Employee Non-Disclosure and Cyber Conduct Agreement. These documents were created to ensure adherence to regulatory requirements while maintaining a professional and consistent brand image. The layout is straightforward, with attention given to detail to ensure clarity and readability.

All of these materials are available in my dedicated GitHub repository, which can be accessed here. This repository contains high-quality images and source files for each of the designs, providing a closer look at the details and design choices involved.
