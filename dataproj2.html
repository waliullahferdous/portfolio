2023 Sales Performance Analysis & Strategic Insights for Market Expansion for MTC

This project is a comprehensive business report that analyzes the 2023 sales performance of Mega Transportation Company (MTC), with the aim of providing strategic insights for market expansion. As the major assignment for the unit BUSA1000 at Macquarie University, the report leverages a Tableau dashboard featuring 15 insightful visualizations to uncover key data trends, such as quarterly sales peaks, bestselling products, top-performing regions, and valuable customer segments.

The analysis reveals several critical findings that are pivotal for MTC's strategic decision-making. Notably, the report highlights significant Q4 sales peaks, indicating the effectiveness of year-end promotions or seasonal demand surges. The analysis also identifies the USA as the leading market, with Spain and France following, emphasizing the importance of these regions for market expansion. Additionally, the top-performing product line, Classic Cars, and key customer segments such as the Euro Shopping Channel are emphasized as substantial contributors to sales. The bestselling product, the S18_3232 model, is highlighted for its significant demand, suggesting opportunities for further investment in this line.

Insights are provided to guide the company in refining product offerings, tailoring marketing strategies, and optimizing regional sales efforts. The report suggests expanding the Classic Cars range, exploring cross-promotional opportunities with other product lines, and focusing on targeted marketing campaigns in top-performing regions to maximize revenue. Moreover, the analysis of customer segments reveals opportunities for nurturing high-value relationships, such as offering customized solutions or volume-based pricing incentives to key clients like Euro Shopping Channel.

Due to academic integrity restrictions posed by Macquarie University, I could not make the repository containing the dashboard and the full report public. However, it can be shared with potential recruiters upon request from here.
