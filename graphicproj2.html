Derma Star Trade International

This project showcases the creation of branding materials for Derma Star Trade International, focusing on the design of a logo and a visiting card. Each element has been meticulously crafted using Canva, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop to provide a professional, cohesive brand identity that effectively represents the company's values and vision.

Logo Design: The logo for Derma Star Trade International embodies the company's focus on quality and professionalism. The design features clean lines and a modern aesthetic, making it versatile for various applications such as digital platforms and printed materials.

Visiting Card Design: The visiting card was designed to maintain consistency with the logo, ensuring that the brand's visual identity is unified across all mediums. It includes essential contact details and a clear, professional layout that leaves a lasting impression. The card's design combines simplicity with elegance, enhancing its visual appeal.

Both of these materials are available in my dedicated GitHub repository, which can be accessed here. The repository contains high-quality images and source files for each design, providing a closer look at the details and design choices involved.
